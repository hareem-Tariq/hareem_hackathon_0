# Plan

## Objective
Develop a comprehensive, bootstrap-friendly growth strategy for Dezignwise that generates 20 qualified leads monthly through targeted outreach, strategic content, and multi-channel positioning—positioning the agency as an authority in AI-powered web design for startups and SaaS companies across US/UK markets.

## Steps
- [ ] **Define and segment target audience** — Create 3 buyer personas (SaaS founders, startup CTOs, small business owners) with specific pain points, budget ranges, and buying triggers; map these to Dezignwise's UI/UX and AI capabilities to identify ideal customer profile (ICP)

- [ ] **Establish multi-channel lead generation system** — Implement organic channels (LinkedIn content strategy: 2-3 posts/week showcasing case studies and AI web design trends; SEO-optimized blog posts on "AI websites for startups"); launch low-cost paid experiments ($500-800/month on LinkedIn ads and Google Ads targeting SaaS keywords); activate freelancing platforms (Upwork, PeoplePerHour) to capture project-based leads and build portfolio proof

- [ ] **Build systematic outreach and nurture funnel** — Create cold email sequences (10-12 emails over 4 weeks) targeting startup founders and CTOs with personalized case studies; use Apollo.io or Hunter.io ($50-100/month) to identify prospects; implement LinkedIn multi-step outreach (connection → message → value-add content); develop email nurture sequence for non-immediate prospects (monthly design tips, industry insights)

- [ ] **Create and distribute authority content** — Publish bi-weekly case study posts on LinkedIn and company blog highlighting client results; create 1-2 monthly YouTube shorts/Loom videos demonstrating Dezignwise's AI website features; develop 4-6 long-form pillar content pieces (e.g., "AI Website Builder Comparison for SaaS," "Modern UI/UX Trends 2024") optimized for organic discovery and backlink potential

- [ ] **Set up lightweight automation and tracking** — Integrate Zapier (free tier) to log inbound leads automatically; use Calendly for self-scheduled demos (reduces sales friction); implement basic CRM (HubSpot free or Notion) to track lead source, stage, and conversion metrics; monitor monthly: lead volume by source, conversion rate, cost per lead, and adjust channels accordingly

## Notes
- **Bootstrap-friendly cost estimate**: ~$150-300/month (tools) + sweat equity; skip expensive agency marketing initially
- **Quick wins**: LinkedIn outreach and case study content generate leads fastest (4-6 weeks to first conversions)
- **Realistic expectation**: 20 leads/month is achievable with 2-3 dedicated hours daily on outreach + content; 5 client conversions assumes 25% close rate (strong for service sales)
- **Risk mitigation**: Prioritize organic + cold outreach first; scale paid ads only after proving channel ROI
- **Key metric to watch**: Cost per qualified lead—if exceeding $50-100 per lead, adjust messaging and targeting
