# Task: Client Growth Strategy for Dezignwise

Create a complete growth strategy for a web design agency (Dezignwise) targeting international clients.

Context:
- Agency specializes in modern UI/UX and AI-powered websites
- Target audience: startups, SaaS companies, and small businesses in US/UK
- Budget is limited (bootstrap stage)

Goals:
- Generate 20 qualified leads per month
- Close at least 5 clients monthly
- Build strong online presence and authority

Requirements:
- Define target audience segments clearly
- Suggest lead generation channels (organic + paid)
- Include outreach strategy (LinkedIn, cold email, freelancing platforms)
- Provide content strategy (what to post and where)
- Include simple funnel (lead → conversion)
- Suggest tools to automate process

Output Format:
- Clear objective
- 3-5 actionable steps
- Practical and realistic execution plan
